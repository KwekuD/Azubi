Why Azubi Africa

People learn best by doing. Our program is not just an endless series of video lessons. It incorporates experiential learning throughout, using interactive labs that put you in control of interesting situations.

Lectures from experienced tutors and mentors

Our experienced tutors and mentors provide personalized lectures and guidance to help you master HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design principles. With hands-on projects and practical assignments, you'll be prepared for success in the fast-paced world of front-end development.

Career coaching by industry experts

In addition to expert lectures, our learners receive career coaching from industry experts who provide personalized guidance on job search strategies, resume building, and interview techniques. With our comprehensive front-end development program, you'll have the skills and support you need to enhance your career.

Only pay back once you get into a job

Looking for a risk-free way to invest in your career? With our Income Share Agreement (ISA), learners only pay back tuition once they secure a job in the industry. Our expert lectures, practical assignments, and personalized career coaching ensure that you're fully equipped to launch your dream career. Don't let tuition costs hold you back - enroll now and invest in your future with confidence!
